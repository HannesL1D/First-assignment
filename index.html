Pasta Bolognese

  • Background
  • Ingredients
  • Instructions

Background

Pasta bolognese is a meat based ragu. As the name suggest the dish origins from the italian city Bologna.
There are endless of variations to the bolognese and the following recipe is a simple classic version.

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 2 onions
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 red chilli
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 500g minced meat
  • 2 cans of tomatoes
  • splash of red/white wine
  • some oregano
  • some basil

Instructions

  1. Put a large cooking pot on high heat.
  2. Add the minced meat and fry until browned (don't overstir).
  3. Reduce the heat and add the finely chopped carrots, onions and celery. Cook until softened.
  4. Make room on the pots bottom and add the garlic and tomato purée, let it fry for a short while (to reduce the sharpness and acidity).
    Then add the chilli.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients, cover with a lid and let it simmer for at least 30 minutes, preferably longer.

Serving

Serve with spaghetti, grated parmesan and fresh basil.

Buon appetito!
